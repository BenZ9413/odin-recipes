Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a noodle dish from Italy. It consists of several layers of noodle with bolognese sauce. In the end you bake it with a layer of cheese.

The romans facilitated a wildly used technique during the antiques to make flat pieces of dough, which were then processed to stripes, which got cooked in the end. These pieces were called lagana, which resembles the origin of the word lasagna.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna plates
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Processed meat
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Take the processed meat and fry it with the onions and the garlic.
  2. Wipe the form with oil.
  3. Add layers of tomato sauce, processed meat and lasagna plates.
  4. Add a layer of cheese.
  5. Put it for 25 minutes into the oven.
  6. Enjoy the perfectly cooked lasagna.
