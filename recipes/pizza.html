Pizza

Description

Pizza is a dish of Italian origin consisting of a usually round, flat base of leavened wheat-based dough topped with tomatoes, cheese and often various other ingredients.

The word "pizza" first appeared in a Latin text from the town of Geata, then still part of the Byzantine Empire, in 997 AD.

Ingredients

  • Olive oil
  • Cornmeal
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Mushrooms
  • Pepperoni, thinly sliced
  • Onios, thinly sliced raw
  • Ham, thinly sliced

Steps

  1. Prepare the pizza dough.
  2. Prepare the different toppings.
  3. Stretch out the dough into a round form.
  4. Spread the tomato sauce and sprinkle with toppings.
  5. Bake the pizza inside of an oven.
  6. Enjoy the delicious pizza fresh from the oven!
