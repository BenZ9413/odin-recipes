Scrambled eggs

Description

Scrambled eggs is a dish made from eggs (usually chicken eggs) stirred, whipped or beaten together whiile being gently heated.

Scrambled eggs probably are the easiest recipe to make; but no pressure.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Salt
  • Milk

Steps

  1. Crack the eggs into a bowl.
  2. Add some salt and pepper and stirr the mixture.
  3. Pour the mixture into a hot pan and add milk, if desired.
  4. Stirr the eggs as they are cooked.
  5. Scrambled eggs are best served hot.
